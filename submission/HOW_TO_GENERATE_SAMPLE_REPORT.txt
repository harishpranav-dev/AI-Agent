HOW TO GENERATE sample_report.pdf
==================================

The sample_report.pdf is the single piece of *evidence* in this submission package —
it proves the system actually produces output end-to-end. Generate it from your own
live deployment so it's authentic.

STEPS:

1. Open your live URL (from live_url.txt) in a browser
2. Navigate to Multi-Agent Studio
3. Enter a strong research goal. Good examples:
   - "Research the latest breakthroughs in quantum computing and their real-world
     applications in 2026"
   - "Analyze current trends in renewable energy adoption globally"
   - "Summarize recent advances in large language models and their impact on
     software engineering"
4. Click Run Agents
5. Wait for the full pipeline to complete (~30 seconds)
6. Click "Export PDF" on the completed report
7. Rename the downloaded file to: sample_report.pdf
8. Place it in this submission/ folder (alongside this file)
9. DELETE this instruction file before zipping the final submission

WHAT TO PICK:

Choose a topic that:
- Has strong, current web results (so the Researcher finds good data)
- Produces a visually clean, multi-section report
- Looks impressive to an examiner scanning the PDF

AVOID topics that:
- Are too narrow (Researcher may find thin data)
- Require specialized knowledge to evaluate
- Are politically sensitive (distracts from the tech)

The PDF should look like a polished, multi-page research report with an executive
summary, structured sections, and cited sources.
